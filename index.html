About me

Product Design Leader focused on SAAS products, Design Systems and Mobile Applications. I strive to be an advocate for human beings in a world of business and technology. I create vision, processes and strategy to lead design teams to delivering efficient, engaging and intuitive products that users love.

I have led multiple design teams to deliver timely user-centerd solutions and design systems. I prioritize my team's well being so we can joyfully deliver quality work and meet our timelines. A balance of genuine care and efficient processes is my secret sauce as a leader.

My past experience as an account manager helps me to be constantly aware of the big picture while paying attention to fine details. I embrace change in technology, design tools and the world as a whole. When life gives me lemons, I make a tasty lemonade :)

I find great peace and joy on white sandy beaches, in Yoga and meditation, cuddling my children, singing and playing my guitar, painting and gardening.

The most important quality about me is: I pour my heart and soul into whatever I choose to do!
