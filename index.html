TouchPlow Android app


TouchPlow is an application that aims to be the Uber for snow plowing services. I Designed the User interface for the application for both iOS and Android platforms. The app is currently available on the app store and was developed by Iversoft Solutions Inc. While designing this application, I learned the nitty-gritty design requirements and specifications of both Apple and Google. Following the platform's design guidelines is essential for the success of any mobile application.

Groovy Music App


I created "Groovy" for the User Experience Design course at Algonquin college. The project started with user reasearch that led to personas, scenarios, paper prototypes, testing, wireframes and ended with a high fidelity visual design and an Invision interactive prototype. The steps taken during this project are the essence of UX Design.

AC Hub App


“ACHub” is an enterprise iPad application used by the AC Hub staff since August 2015 at their various stations throughout Algonquin College. I individually designed and developed this app in the summer of 2015, which was a huge learning opportunity. The app provides quick tools and access to information that allows the staff to assist students visiting the AC Hub desks. I chose Ionic Framework to give a native iOS look and feel to a Hybrid app.

SitterAdvantage App

SitterAdvantage is a mobile application for The Prez inc. aimed to provide effective tools for young baby sitters. I was the team lead for the project and was involved in every stage of the product's life cycle including research, design, prototyping and development. The application is developed for iOS and Android using Angular JS and Ionic.
