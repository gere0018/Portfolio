About

I am a User Experience Designer and Front End Developer committed to creating user-centered applications. Adept in researching, identifying the needs of the target audience and converting them into user-friendly interfaces.

Before delving into technology, I invested a large portion of my life in deep learning and self-exploration through mediation and yoga. My Inner Journey and extensive travels around the world gave me a wide exposure to life and people from different backgrounds and cultures. I understand human beings, and I am constantly learning new technologies that empower me to create what they need. This is my superpower when designing and developing an application.

Groovy Music App


I created "Groovy" for the User Experience Design course at Algonquin college. The project started with user reasearch that led to personas, scenarios, paper prototypes, testing, wireframes and ended with a high fidelity visual design and an Invision interactive prototype. The steps taken during this project are the essence of UX Design.
