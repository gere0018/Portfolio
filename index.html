About me

Product Design Leader focused on SAAS products, Design Systems and Mobile Applications. I strive to be an advocate for human beings in a world of business and technology. I create vision, processes and strategy to lead design teams to deliver efficient, engaging and intuitive products that users love.

I prioritize my teams' well-being so we can joyfully deliver quality work and meet our timelines. A balance of genuine care and efficient processes is my secret sauce as a leader.

I am constantly aware of the big picture while paying attention to fine details. I embrace change in technology, design tools and the world as a whole. When life gives me lemons, I make a tasty lemonade :)

I am also a Design instructor at Algonquin College, Ottawa. Teaching and mentoring is my (Ikigai), my biggest gift and joy that I recently developed into a Youtube channel to make design knowledge open source and offer guidance to those seeking it.

I find great peace and joy on white sandy beaches, in Yoga and meditation, cuddling my children, singing and playing my guitar, painting and gardening.

The most important quality about me is: I pour my heart and soul into whatever I choose to do!

Experience

  • October 2022 - Present

    Cvent - Canada

    Principal Product Designer

    For the last two years, I have simultaneously lead multiple teams of product designers at Cvent to deliver user-centered solutions and a cohesive design system for the whole suite of Cvent products. I am proud to have established solid processes for team efficiency and brought agile methodologies and “Jira software” to design teams. These processes significantly increased design efficiency and collaboration with engineers and product managers by brining visibility to design tasks and needs to all stakeholders. I work closely with leaders of product and engineering to create strategies for serving our clients’ needs. I am also directly involved in applying the latest Figma technology to our design system library to enable complex theming capabilities and ensure the creation of reusable and scalable components. I am constantly focused on the big picture ensuring our products are consistent, usable and accessible.

  • 2017 - Present

    Algonquin College - Canada

    Design Professor

    I teach the UX and UI courses as part of the “Mobile Applications Design & Development" program. I also redesigned the content for the UI course in 2024 to include the latest industry standards expected from designers. Teaching design is very fulfilling for me. My passion for design and experience in the field help me provide students with practical knowledge and techniques to be successful designers and developers.

  • May 2018 - October 2022

    Quest Software - Canada

    Senior User Experience Designer

    I've lead a team of designers to create solutions and a comprehensive design system that powered the On Demand SAAS products. I worked closely with product owners, engineers and designers to create Quest On Demand and deliver strategy for clients' needs. I have created and maintained multiple products that enabled users to perform complex administrative tasks like user and content migrations, data recovery, groups and license management.

  • November 2016 - May 2018

    Spiria Digital - Canada

    UX Designer & Consultant

    Successfully completed a multitude of mobile and web applications for different service industries varying in scope and complexity. The fun of working in Spiria was the ability to see the concrete impact and progress of a solution from beginning to end. I loved designing applications that empowered small businesses to be more successful in their domain. I lead client discovery workshops, gathered requirements, presented solutions and kept clients updated throughout the project lifecycle. Simultaneously, I worked with developers, product managers and designers to deliver design solutions. I conducted user interviews and market research, created low and high fidelity designs and validated success with usability testing sessions.
