Project Title

AC Hub App

I created this app in the summer of 2015 when I worked with the AC Hub. As staff we needed to assist students with all kinds of tasks and questions. I wanted this app to be a tool to facilitate the job of the AC Hub staff by creating one space that acts as a resource for all the questions we received. I used Ionic to create a native iOS feel for the application that was targeted towards iPad minis.

SitterAdvantage App

This app was our graduation project during the last semester of the Mobile Applications Design and Development program at Algonquin College. Our client was Brian Presly of "The Prez inc." and he wanted to develop an application that can be a tool to assist young baby sitters. I was the team lead for a group of 4 designers and developers and was the contact person coordinating with the client.
